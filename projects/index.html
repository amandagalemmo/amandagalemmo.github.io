My Projects

Below are some projects that I've worked on, either independently or collaboratively, and where they live on the web.

Minesweeper is a Roguelike

Born from the idea that Minesweeper fits the definition of a roguelike as laid out in Wikipedia, I created a simple dungeon-crawler-esque verison of Minesweeper where you are just some little guy in a procedurally-generated world. Currently in progress, with some visual bugs that need to be ironed out + some feature additions planned for the future. Play it here!

Tuesday Night Project - A Hug Vibes Care Package

I am the current website maintainer for Tuesday Night Project, an Asian American grassroots and volunteer-based organization fiercely devoted to bridging communities by providing programming and interactive spaces for people to connect through artistic expression and strong, creative, community partnerships. As a fun way to connect with our community during COVID, the team created the "Hug Vibes Care Package", a shipped box with various items relating to Little Tokyo and the TNP community. I designed and programmed in collaboration the landing page for the project, linked above.
